Bet United Social

Company: United Interactive Games

Duration: July 2024 – Present

Project Overview

Bet United Social is a feature-rich social casino game where players purchase chips, earn SC Coins, and can convert SC Coins into real money upon reaching the threshold. The game fosters player engagement through friend requests, gifting, social tournaments, and a diverse collection of 100+ games from 3+ game providers. It offers exciting casino-style gameplay, including slots, poker, and blackjack, along with hourly and daily bonuses to increase retention and daily logins. With in-game purchases and virtual rewards, the platform ensures an immersive and competitive experience across iOS, Android, and Desktop.

Role & Responsibilities

  • ✅ Performed API testing using Postman to validate backend responses and ensure seamless transactions.
  • ✅ Designed and executed test cases, maintaining detailed documentation in JIRA.
  • ✅ Led User Acceptance Testing (UAT) to verify game functionality before release.
