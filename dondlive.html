Deal Or No Deal Live - Social Casino

Company: Ipro Games

Duration: 2022 – 2023

Project Overview

Deal Or No Deal Live is a social casino game where players purchase chips, engage with others, and enjoy various casino-style games, including slots, poker, and blackjack. The game offers an immersive experience with in-game purchases and virtual rewards, ensuring engaging and competitive gameplay.

Role & Responsibilities

  • ✅ Designed and executed test cases for comprehensive test coverage.
  • ✅ Conducted manual & exploratory testing to detect critical bugs.
  • ✅ Performed regression testing to ensure system stability.
  • ✅ Managed and tracked defects in JIRA, ensuring clear documentation.
  • ✅ Collaborated closely with developers & product managers for quality assurance.

Tools & Technologies Used

Test Management: JIRA

Database: MySQL, PostgreSQL

Challenges & Solutions

A major challenge was optimizing the game for lag-free performance. I worked with developers to identify performance bottlenecks and enhance responsiveness, ensuring smooth gameplay across different devices.

Outcome & Impact

  • ✔ Reduced critical bugs by 60% before launch, enhancing stability.
  • ✔ Decreased crash rates by 25%, improving user experience.
  • ✔ Ensured seamless cross-platform performance on iOS & Android.
